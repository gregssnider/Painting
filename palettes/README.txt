Using the skin palettes.

For pale skin, you will typically use the following values:

    8  highlight
    7  center light
    6  local color
    5  half tone (part of the light)
    4  reflected light (in the shadow)
    3  core shadow


For dark skin, use the dark skin palette with the following values:

    6  highlight
    5  center light
    4  local color
    3  half tone (part of the light)
    2  reflected light (in the shadow)
    1  core shadow


For a simpler painting model, use these values for Caucasian skin (values on the
11 tone Munsell scale):

    9  highlight
    7  local color
    5  half tone (part of the light)
    3  reflected light (in the shadow)
    1  core shadow
